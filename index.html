Summary
This simulated dashboard transforms SAP-style material, inventory and planning data into a live inventory intelligence cockpit. It supports material planners by highlighting stockout risk, excess inventory, demand-supply gaps, reorder priorities and planning accuracy trends. The dashboard demonstrates how structured reporting can improve decision-making in material planning, inventory control and production supply.
